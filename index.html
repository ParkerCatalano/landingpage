Personal Life/Background

Since I was very young I have been extremely motivated to draw and make art with various mediums. I eventually got into an art High School in San Francisco where I studied visual art for three years. Although my college career ended up being a Literary one, I am still as fond of visual art as I ever was, and feel that I will be able to incorporate my skills into the programming and development world in a design capacity. I have had quite a few jobs, not many of which utilized my skills in the visual or programming fields which is the primary reason why I decided to enroll at epicodus. I can't wait to see what opportunities arise from this!

Programming Experience

I began working at a startup called MOG founded by my close family friend David Hyman, Co-founder of the track-finding software Gracenote in 2006. It was a summer job and I was doing mostly QA and beta testing for the site's various widgets and extensions, as well as some blog post editing. It was technically a paid internship, and I was very fortunate to be able to experience a workplace like that at my age. It taught me a lot of very valuable lessons about the strtup world and the problems that face even a well funded website.
